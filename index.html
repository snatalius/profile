About Samuel

I am currently a MSc student in TU Delft, The Netherlands, majoring in Complex System Engineering & Management (CoSEM). Previously, I was a senior analyst at a global consulting company. In a spare time, Samuel enjoys doing his hobby in music, especially singing and piano. He also enjoys traveling around the world and understanding various cultures.
